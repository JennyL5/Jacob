Home About Recipes

Welcome to our website on cooking with your kids. We provide a platform for parents and children to bond over cooking.
This will also help children understand more about their food consumption, especially with the increase in obesity in recent years.
With these recipes we hope that children become more confident and creative when cooking, and reduce the amount of junk foods and take-out
consumed. This will then lead to a healthier and happier lifestyle for them and everyone surrounding them. This also provides an opportunity
for the parents and children to bond, as well as developing key skills that can be applied in other aspects of their lives.
