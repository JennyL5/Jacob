Cheese toastie

Home About Recipes

Ingredients

You will need:
25g (2oz) butter,
4 slices wholemeal bread,
100g (3oz) cheddar cheese, grated.

Method:

1. The child can butter the bread.
2. The child can arrange the cheese over half of the slices, buttered side down.
3. The child can cover the remaining slices, buttered side up.
4. The parent should preheat a frying pan and cook the sandwiches for 2 mins on each side, until golden brown and the cheese has melted.
5. The parent can cut into triangles and serve.
